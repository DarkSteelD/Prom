About SeaDocs

SeaDocs is a document management web application that helps manufacturers and consumers of industrial products to efficiently store and manage all their documents in one place. Our platform is designed to simplify document management, saving you time and effort in searching for and retrieving important documents.

Our platform is cloud-based, so you can access your documents anytime and anywhere with an internet connection. We also provide features like advanced search and filtering options, document tracking, and user management to enhance the overall document management experience.

Features

Cloud-based

Access your documents anytime and anywhere with an internet connection.

Advanced Search

Effortlessly search for and retrieve important documents with advanced search and filtering options.

Document Tracking

Track the status and progress of your documents from upload to approval and beyond.

User Management

Easily manage users and their permissions to access and edit documents in your account.
